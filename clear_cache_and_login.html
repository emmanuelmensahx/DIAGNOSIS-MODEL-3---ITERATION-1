If you're experiencing login issues, it might be due to browser cache. Follow these steps:

Manual Cache Clear:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac) to hard refresh
  2. Or press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Right-click the refresh button and select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
  4. Or go to Application/Storage tab and clear Local Storage
